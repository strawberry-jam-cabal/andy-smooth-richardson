Bio
Student of smooth jazz since 1994. I've been writing, recording and composing smooth jazz music since before I can remember. Described by the Halifax Examiner as "one of Halifax's most admired neo-bop jazz artists". For bookings please use email, and for information about past and upcoming tours see below.
